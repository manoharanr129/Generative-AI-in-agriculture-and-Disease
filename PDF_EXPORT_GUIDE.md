## Tips for Best PDF Quality

- Use **Chrome** or **Edge** browser for best results
- Ensure **"Background graphics"** is enabled in print settings
- Select **"Save as PDF"** not "Print to PDF" if available
- Use **Portrait** orientation
- Keep **margins at default** (usually 1cm or 0.4in)
